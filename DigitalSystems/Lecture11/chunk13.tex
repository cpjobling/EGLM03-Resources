The transform of the digital step signal is
\begin{eqnarray}\label{eq:l10e6}
  V = E &=& \sum_{k=0}^{\infty}\epsilon_k z^{-k}\\
        &=& \sum_{k=0}^{\infty} z^{-k}\nonumber \\
        &=& 1 + z^{-1} + z^{-2} + z^{-3} + \cdots z^{-n} + \cdots \nonumber\\
        &=& \frac{1}{1-z^{-1}} = \frac{z}{z-1}.
\end{eqnarray}
