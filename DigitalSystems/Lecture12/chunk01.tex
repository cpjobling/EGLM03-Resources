\heading{Relationship of $z$ to $s$}
From the preceding arguments
  \begin{equation}
    \label{eq:l11e1}
    z^{-1} = e^{-sT}
  \end{equation}
  That is
  \begin{equation}
    \label{eq:l11e2}
    z=e^{sT}
  \end{equation}
or
\begin{equation}
  \label{eq:l11e3}
  s=\frac{1}{T}\ln z
\end{equation}
