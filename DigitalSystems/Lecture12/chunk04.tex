The sum of these two signals is
\begin{eqnarray*}
  p(t) &=& v_k \epsilon(t - kT) - v_k \epsilon(t - \{k+1\}T)\\
       &=& v_k\left[\epsilon(t - kT) - \epsilon(t - \{k+1\}T)\right]
\end{eqnarray*}
So for the sequence:
\begin{equation}
  \label{eq:l11e5}
  v(t) = \sum_{k=0}^{\infty} v_k\left[\epsilon(t - kT) - \epsilon(t - \{k+1\}T)\right]
\end{equation}
