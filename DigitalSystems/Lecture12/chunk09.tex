Completing the analysis, we can show that
$$y(nT)\approx y(nT-T) + \frac{T}{2}\{u(nT-T)+u(T)\}$$
which on taking z-transforms gives
$$Y(z)=z^{-1}Y(z)+\frac{T}{2}\{z^{-1}U(z)+U(z)\}$$
thus
\begin{equation}
\frac{Y(z)}{U(z)}=D(z)=\frac{T}{2}\left\{ {\frac{{1 + {z^{ - 1}}}}{{1 - {z^{ - 1}}}}} \right\}.
\end{equation}
