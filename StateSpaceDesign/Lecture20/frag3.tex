Suppose the desired closed loop poles are to be at $p_1$, $p_2$, $\cdots$, $p_n$, then the desired CE is:
\begin{equation}
	\label{eq:2}
	\alpha_c(s) = (s-p_1)(s-p_2)\cdots(s-p_n)=0
\end{equation} 

Equation (\ref{eq:2}) is multiplied out to a polynomial in $s$.
