	\item Ackermann's formula is useful for SISO systems of order  $n\le 10$.
	\item $\mathcal{C}$ becomes numerically inaccurate for large $n$.
	\item The system must be \emph{controllable} for $\mathcal{C}^{-1}$ to exist.
\end{itemize}
